XKCD 149, AKA Sandwich, is justly treasured as a classic of nerd humor, but there are plenty of other potential punchlines.

It started with Jack William Bell's tweets yesterday:

Make me a sandwich! What? No! Sudo make me a sandwich You are a sandwich.

— jackwilliambell (@jackwilliambell) October 18, 2015

Make me a sandwich! What? No! Sudo make me a sandwich Invalid password. Invalid password. Invalid password. Invalid password. . . .

— jackwilliambell (@jackwilliambell) October 18, 2015

And then others got in on the action:

.@jackwilliambell make me a sandwich no sudo make me a sandwich Siri. My name is Siri.

— daryl (@easthollow) October 18, 2015

@jackwilliambell @doctorow sudo apt-get install make sandwich

— Party Hard (@Adef187) October 18, 2015

@jackwilliambell @doctorow Make me a sandwich! What? No! sudo make me a sandwich! make: *** No rule to make target `me'. Stop.

— Geoff Robbins (@_TheGeoff) October 18, 2015

I'm sure you've got some punchlines of your own to suggest.
